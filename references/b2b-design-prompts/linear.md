# Internal Style Prompt: Efficient Workbench

Design a polished B2B workbench that feels fast, structured, and quietly premium. The interface should support repeated operational work: scanning, comparing, filtering, editing, assigning, and resolving items without visual noise.

Use a compact layout with strong alignment, crisp table/list density, clear status language, and subtle surface hierarchy. Prefer restrained neutral surfaces, precise borders, small but legible typography, concise labels, and confident spacing. Primary actions should feel clear but not loud; secondary actions should remain available without competing.

The mood is focused, exact, modern, and calm. Avoid marketing decoration. Let the page feel impressive through information architecture, state design, keyboard-friendly controls, saved views, command-like filters, and meaningful operational data.

Translate this into Design Anchor tokens for compact density, clean radius, subtle borders, clear focus states, and high scanability.
